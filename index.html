About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Operations and Business Analytics at New York University Shanghai. I received my Ph.D. in Decision Sciences from Fuqua School of Business, Duke University. Prior to joining NYU Shanghai, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Operations Management area at Booth School of Business, University of Chicago.

Research Interests

Broadly, I am interested in developing methodologies for dynamic optimization under uncertainty and prescriptive analytics, and applying these to improve the operations of modern marketplaces.
